Pink Shade Multi-Color Designer Silk Ready to Wear Lehenga Choli

$180

Lehenga choli is an ensemble worn by women across the country and cultures.
It holds a deep cultural significance and reflects the country’s rich diversity.
This timeless piece always finds a place in a woman’s special-occasion wardrobe.
The intricate embroidery and bright colours make it ravishing.
When shopping for a lehenga, finding your colour is crucial.
Your skin undertones, the time of the occasion and your taste will help determine the colour.
As for the fabric, the type of occasion plays a major role. Go for traditional heavy material like silk if it's a wedding and opulence is what you wish to exude. But if it’s a semi-formal, more intimate event, perhaps a lightweight fabric like Organza will work the best.
