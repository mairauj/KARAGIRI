Lehenga Design

Lehenga choli, gagra choli or ghagra choli, which is also known as chaniya choli in locally and लहंगा. lehenga choli is a traditional Indian attire. In Indian history, it was considered as royal Indian attire and worn by every woman. Nowadays it is worn at functions and occasions only. Lehenga choli is a Combination of Lehenga or Ghagra and choli (blouse) with Dupatta. Women’s lehengas have been modernized and differently designed in every state of India. Lehenga is not just restricted only for India, women all over the world adopting this Indian lehenga choli apparel.
